Research Interests

My research focuses on nuclear radiation detection, shielding protection, and Monte Carlo simulation methods for nuclear engineering applications.

☆

辐射探测 Radiometry

Research on advanced radiation detection techniques including scintillation detectors, semiconductor detectors, and gamma spectroscopy for nuclear safety and radiation monitoring applications.

☆

屏蔽防护 Shielding

Design and optimization of radiation shielding materials and structures for nuclear reactors, medical facilities, and space applications using advanced simulation and experimental validation.

☆

蒙卡仿真 Monte Carlo

Monte Carlo particle transport simulation using MCNP, Geant4, and FLUKA for nuclear reactor physics, radiation dosimetry, and shielding design optimization.
